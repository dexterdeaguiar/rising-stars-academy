About Rising Stars Academy

Discover our rich history, core values, and commitment to nurturing future leaders.

Our History

Established in 2009, Rising Stars Academy began as a small educare centre with a big vision: to make quality education accessible in a nurturing environment. Over the years, the school has grown from a handful of toddlers into a comprehensive educational institution serving learners from 3 months through to Grade 12.

Today, Rising Stars Academy operates three specialised schools on one campus — Little Stars Educare, Rising Stars Primary, and Rising Stars High — each with dedicated staff, tailored programmes, and age-appropriate facilities. Our growth reflects the trust placed in us by hundreds of families.

Yes. The live site will include dedicated online enrollment forms for Educare, Primary, and High School.
